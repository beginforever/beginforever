Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account? +
Visit beginforever.app and click "Create Account". Enter your email and set a password (minimum 6 characters). Once registered, you'll complete a 6-step profile setup including your personal details, faith preferences, photos, and government ID verification.
Is Begin Forever free to join? +
Yes! Creating an account and setting up your profile is completely free. We offer subscription plans (Monthly ₹499, Quarterly ₹1,299, Yearly ₹2,599) for premium features like unlimited messaging and priority matching. First 300 founding members get 1 week free.
Which religions and denominations are supported? +
Begin Forever welcomes people of all faiths — Christian, Hindu, Muslim, Sikh, Jain, Buddhist, and others. Within each religion, you can filter by denomination or community. For Christians, we support Catholic, Protestant, Pentecostal, Baptist, CSI/CNI, Methodist, Orthodox, Mar Thoma, and many more.

Profile & Verification

Why do I need to submit a Government ID? +
We verify every member's identity to ensure 100% genuine profiles. This eliminates fake accounts, bots, and catfishing. Your ID is encrypted, stored securely, and NEVER shown to other members. Only our admin team reviews it for verification.
Which IDs are accepted? +
We accept Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, and Passport. Any valid government-issued photo ID works.
How long does profile approval take? +
Profile approvals are currently done manually by our team. Most profiles are approved within 24-48 hours. You'll be notified once your profile is live.
Can I edit my profile after submitting? +
Yes! Once approved, go to the Profile tab in the app and click "Edit Profile". You can update your bio, education, occupation, phone number, and photos anytime.

Matching & Messaging

How does matching work? +
Browse verified profiles in the Discover tab. Use filters for religion, denomination, age, and city. When you find someone interesting, click "Express Interest". If they accept your interest, you become a mutual match and can start messaging.
Can I control who contacts me? +
Yes! In your Faith Preferences, you can choose which religions can send you interests and which profiles you want to browse. You have full control over your experience.
Is messaging private? +
Absolutely. Messages are private between you and your match. No one else — not even admins — can read your conversations. Messaging is only available between mutual matches (both parties accepted interest).

Payments & Subscription

What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major payment methods via Razorpay — Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking, and Wallets. All payments are secure and encrypted.
Can I cancel my subscription? +
Yes, you can cancel anytime. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the billing period. No refunds for partial periods. Contact us at info@beginforever.in for cancellation.
