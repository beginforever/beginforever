Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our highest priority

"The Lord is my rock, my fortress and my deliverer; my God is my rock, in whom I take refuge."
— Psalm 18:2

At Begin Forever, your safety is at the heart of everything we do. We've built a platform rooted in faith, trust, and accountability. Here are our guidelines to help you stay safe while finding your God-ordained match.

🛡️ How We Protect You

100% ID Verification

Every member must submit a valid government-issued photo ID before their profile is approved. Our admin team manually reviews each profile to ensure authenticity. No anonymous profiles are allowed.

Admin-Approved Profiles

No profile goes live without manual review and approval by our team. We check for completeness, authenticity, and appropriateness before making any profile visible to other members.

Secure In-App Messaging

All communication happens within our secure platform. You never need to share your personal phone number, email, or social media until you're comfortable and ready.

Photo Privacy Controls

You control who sees your photos. You can choose to keep photos private and only share them with specific members upon request.

✅ Safety Tips for Members

Protect Your Personal Information

  • Never share your home address, workplace address, or financial information with someone you haven't met in person
  • Don't share passwords or bank details with anyone
  • Be cautious about sharing your phone number — use our in-app messaging first

Take Your Time

  • Don't rush into sharing personal details or meeting in person
  • Get to know the person through in-app messaging first
  • Involve your family and trusted friends/pastors in the process
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels wrong, it probably is

Meeting in Person

  • Always meet in a public place for the first few meetings
  • Tell a family member or friend where you're going and who you're meeting
  • Arrange your own transportation — don't depend on the other person
  • Consider bringing a family member along for the first meeting
  • Don't consume alcohol or anything that impairs your judgement

Red Flags to Watch For

  • Asking for money or financial help
  • Refusing to video call or meet in person after extended messaging
  • Pressuring you to move communication off the platform
  • Inconsistent stories about their life, family, or background
  • Pressuring you for personal photos or inappropriate content
  • Making you feel uncomfortable or guilty
  • Asking you to keep the relationship a secret from your family

🚨 Reporting & Support

If you encounter any suspicious behaviour, harassment, or feel unsafe:

  • Report the profile — use the report button on any profile or message
  • Block the person — you can block anyone at any time
  • Contact us — email us at info@beginforever.in or call +91 97000 25345

We take every report seriously and will investigate within 24 hours. We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, abuse, fraud, and fake profiles.
