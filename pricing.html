Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Begin Forever

What makes Begin Forever different from other matrimony platforms? ▼
Begin Forever is India's first 100% ID-verified matrimony platform open to all faiths. Every member must submit a valid government ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving Licence) and have their profile manually approved by our team before it goes live. This means zero fake profiles, zero bots, and zero spam. Whether you're Hindu, Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Jain, Buddhist or any other faith — Begin Forever is built for you. Our faith-first matching considers your religion, denomination, and personal values, not just appearance.
How does the verification process work? ▼
After signing up, you'll create your profile and upload a government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, or Passport). Our admin team manually verifies every profile within 24–48 hours. We check that your name, photo, and details match your ID. Once approved, your profile becomes visible to other verified members. Your ID document is stored securely and is never shared with other members — it's only used for verification purposes.
Is my personal information safe? ▼
Absolutely. Your privacy is our highest priority. All data is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption. Your phone number and email are never shown to other members — all communication happens through our secure in-app messaging. Your photos have privacy controls — you can choose to keep them private and share only on request. Your government ID is stored securely and used only for verification. We never sell or share your data with third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
Can my family help manage my profile? ▼
Yes! We understand that in the Indian community, finding a life partner is often a family journey. You can register on behalf of your son, daughter, brother, sister, or other relative. The registration form lets you specify who you're registering for. Family members can help manage the profile, review matches, and be part of the conversation — because we believe marriage is a commitment that involves families, not just individuals.
What faiths and denominations are supported? ▼
Begin Forever welcomes members from all faiths — Hindu, Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Jain, Buddhist and others. For Christian members, we support all denominations including Catholic, Protestant, Pentecostal, Baptist, CSI/CNI, Methodist, Seventh-day Adventist, Orthodox, Mar Thoma, Brethren, Lutheran, Presbyterian, Anglican, Non-Denominational, and more. You can filter matches by faith and denomination, and set your preference for same-faith or inter-faith matches. Our goal is to unite hearts across all communities.
What does the 6-Month plan offer vs Quarterly? ▼
The 6-Month plan (₹2,599) gives you 6 months of full access at a single price — saving you ₹1,395 compared to paying monthly for the same period. All features are identical across every plan. It's the best choice for members who want extended access without multiple renewals and at the lowest per-month cost.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no lock-in contracts or cancellation fees. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to your plan's features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your profile will remain on the platform but with limited free-tier access. You can reactivate your subscription anytime. If you wish to delete your account entirely, you can do so from your profile settings, and all your data will be removed within 30 days.
